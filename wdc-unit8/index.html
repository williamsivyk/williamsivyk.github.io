About.

LAUNCH is an incubator space and professional development training program designed for highly motivated emerging architects. At LAUNCH, recently graduation architecture students will join a collaborative network matching innovative materials, technology, products, and ideas with the resources equipped to turn a vision into a reality and introduce the students to the architecture industry. Students and industry experts combine their respective skills and knowledge in order to research, develop, and test the future of design deeply rooted in the architecture discipline.

LAUNCH

An incubator for architecture research and development

Process.

LAUNCH provides a holistic approach to architectural design over the course of three months. Members will take ideas from the initial discovery to a physical prototype. Research and development is supported by the LAUNCH community of industry professionals and works in conjunction with comprehensive training intended to give young architects the ability to work on exciting projects and gain access to a breadth of industry knowledge.

Events.

Leveraging the architecture community of professionals, advisors, and affiliates, LAUNCH is dedicated to the mentorship of architecture students and fostering innovative ideas. At the center of LAUNCH is a three-month professional development program that consists of various collaborative touch points for both architects and industry partners.

Criteria

  • -Individuals and small teams of 4-6 people are eligible.
  • -Full-time membership requires participation in the professional development program for the three month duration.
  • -Part-time membership is available for six-week terms.
  • -Membership is open to architecture students entering the final year of their undergraduate program or students who have recently graduated within the previous academic year.
  • -Organizations are eligible to sponsor small teams. Each individual within the submitted team must meet the incubator criteria and apply separately.
