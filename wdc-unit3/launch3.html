LAUNCH

Making the transition from a.school to a.work

LAUNCH is a co-working space and professional development program designed for highly motivated emerging architects and industry professionals. LAUNCH provides a holistic approach to architectural design in conjunction with a comprehensive training program that consists of various collaborative touch points for both architects and industry partners.

  • Classes and Workshops
  • Peer-to-Peer Sessions
  • Pin Ups
  • Design Dash
  • Mentorship
  • Lunch and Learns
  • LAUNCH Showcase

For additional information, contact the LAUNCH Community.
